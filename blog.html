A tiny snack which takes a great place in Sadhya, that is a banana chip or kaya varathathu.

It is one of my most loved snacks. During my childhood, we bought it from a store. But nowadays we make our own because we got the secret recipe of this crispy banana chips. Usually, we make a bulk amount of chips at a time and store it in a container and used as a teatime snack or every time snacks. This recipe is just as easy as that-thinly sliced raw nendran banana is deep-fried in the coconut oil and seasoned with saltwater, turmeric powder, black pepper and asafoetida. Let's bring back my college memories. I have two friends from my region. We studied in the same college at Chennai then whenever we come for holidays, my college friends used to request us to bring these bananas chip because it tastes better and they never have the same taste as anywhere. After every holiday, we got the banana chips for there 6.

Best ice cream rolls

My experiments usually start with depending on the stuff we had at home. So, when I notice the fresh cream and pomegranate, it triggered an idea in my mind to do an ice cream rolls. I don't have any prior experience of making ice cream rolls, but, I had a chance to witnessed an ice cream shop. It is fun to watch it doing.But here I don't have any proper utensils to do that.But somehow I could do that with household things and set it in the freezer around eight hours.Then, the next I tried to roll, but it didn't come out properly, and it melted. After That, again I kept it in the freezer, and I made ice cream slices with that.It was a random recipe that turned out to be superb
Further, I made a blunder here instead of a spoon have used a fork that's made it more awful, but still, I'm okay with what I did in composition. It doesn't matter if we made a blunder on the initial stage, we can learn through it. And every picture teaches you different lessons.
